Building your PVS-14

×

When people think of night vision or contemplate jumping into this world, it is generally accepted as fact that night vision capabilities are pricey. And that is correct, buying a single PVS-14 with 3rd generation tubes cost anywhere between $3.2-$4K depending on the image intensifiers used.

What people are NOT aware of is the fact that just like building either a gaming PC or a budget AR or Glock, you can save a significant amount of money by sourcing individual parts and building/assembling it yourself.
